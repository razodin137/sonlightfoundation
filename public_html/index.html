About Us

The Sunlight Foundation exists to spread the message of Christ's love and hope to the tribal communities of Naan province. Our mission is to empower these communities through faith, education, and support, building a sustainable future for generations to come.

Why Naan Province?

Naan Province is home to vibrant tribal communities, but many face deep challenges. With few resources and limited access to faith-based support, we believe it is a fertile ground for Christ's light to shine. We are committed to helping transform these communities, starting with the power of the gospel.

Our Work in Naan

We're already on the ground in Naan, working to establish relationships with the people and build connections through practical outreach. Our team is focused on building relationships, providing educational resources, and organizing community events that allow us to share the gospel in relevant ways.

How You Can Help

We need your support to make this mission a success. Here's how you can help:

  • Pray for the people of Naan.
  • Donate to help us fund outreach efforts, education, and community development.
  • Volunteer your skills and time to join us in person or remotely.

Together, we can make a difference!
